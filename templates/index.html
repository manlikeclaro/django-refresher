About Politrack Africa.


Politrack Africa is a private limited company incorporated under the Companies Act 2015. The company specializes in gathering and analyzing data to provide economic, social and political insights or solutions to various industries and clients. Our company is staffed with highly skilled and competent professionals with high level experience in the fields of statistics, data analysis, and market research.

Our Vision

To be a leading research and survey company, recognized for our exceptional quality and insightful analysis, enabling our clients to make informed decisions that drive growth and success.



Our Mission

To provide accurate, comprehensive, and timely research and survey services to our clients. We strive to deliver actionable insights that inform and empower our clients to make data-driven decisions that improve their businesses and the communities they serve. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and ethical conduct in all of our work.

Our Services

Market Research

We conduct in-depth studies of market trends, consumer behavior, and competitor analysis to help clients make informed decisions about their products and services.

Public opinion polls

We conduct polls to gather data on public opinion and attitudes towards specific issues, policies, and candidates.

Employee engagement surveys

We work with organizations to measure employee satisfaction and engagement, identify areas for improvement, and develop strategies to improve workplace culture and productivity.

Data Analysis

We offer data analysis services to help clients make sense of complex data sets and turn data into actionable insights.

Customer Satisfaction Surveys

We design and implement surveys to gauge customer satisfaction and identify areas for improvement in customer service and product quality.

Focus Groups

We organize and facilitate focus group sessions to gather qualitative data on consumer opinions, preferences, and behaviors.

Online Surveys

We design and implement online surveys to gather data from a large and diverse audience quickly and efficiently.

Customized Research

We work with clients to design customized research studies to meet their specific needs and objectives.
